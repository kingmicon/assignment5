In CSS we have what we call BOX MODEL, which is divided into three part; MARGIN, BORDER and PADDING. For every section in HTML it is contained in box

MARGIN

The margin is the space the whole body of the HTML and the body section.

BORDER

The border is the space between the margin and the body of the section box.

PADDING

The padding is the space between the Border and the main body of the section.
